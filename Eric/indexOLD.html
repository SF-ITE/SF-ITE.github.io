Hi, I'm Eric. I am a software engineer and developer for SF-ITE.com in San Francisco. I write games and applications for Android, Linux, and Windows. Fix this line

 I have been coding since the summer of 1986, when I put my dirt bike into a tree and broke my leg. While everyone I knew was tapping kegs down at the beach, I was in my room tapping away on a second-gen TRS-80 Color Computer with a new copy of Color BASIC. I have been writing code ever since.

 I know, all coders have some version of this story, the moment they fell in love with conditional control structures or some such, but I sometimes wonder what would have happened had I not gotten up close and personal with that oak.

 So, I wound up at UGA for my BS in Computer Science, and then wandered over to Georgia Tech for their graduate program. After that, I worked for IBM and Peachtree in Atlanta. After the first .com bubble burst, I received an offer to work for an American contractor in Australia. I wound up working in Canberra for ComSuper, Australia’s (kind of) version of Social Security. I did that for two long years.

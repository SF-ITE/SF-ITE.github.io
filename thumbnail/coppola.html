Francis Ford Coppola Winery

300 Via Archimedes, Geyserville, California

Coppola Winery, Founded 2006

These photos are from a visit in the early Spring of 2021. It was a mild, sunny day, with temperatures in the mid 60's.

188 Acres of Gorgeous Vineyards

Visitors are not really supposed to walk in the vineyards proper, but there was almost no one else there and the staff were chill.

First Glass of Wine; 11 am

There is something about gentle rolling hills, soft breezes, and attentive staff in white linens that makes day drinking positively acceptable.

Not Much Going On in March

As pretty as it looks, there is not much happening in March. I wound up coming back in September, but that is another story.

Hello? Where is Everyone?

Wandered around inside, no sign of other visitors or staff. Not even a security camera, and that is the desk from The Godfather in the distance!

Because California...

Of course there is a pool. How did I not know there was going to be a pool. More wine on the patio. Just after noon.

1/4 Mile Walk Through the Vines

My first time doing that. Solitude. Wind. Crows. A cool breeze offsetting the warm sun. Lavender on the wind.

The Upstairs Tasting Room

"Take the damn picture so I can get back to the wine!" For reasons I do not remember, there is a 3/4 scale pirate ship up there.

The Godfather Memorabilia

It is odd to see the script for one of the greatest movies of all time in a small glass case in an empty room.

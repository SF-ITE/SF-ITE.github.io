The Hanna Winery

Highway 128 Healdsburg, California

Bordeaux Time!

88 acres planted with Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Malbec, and Petit Verdot. The soil and climate are ideal for red Bordeaux varieties.

Les jardins d'Hanna

The gardens on this property are sublime. A great stop off on the way to Mendocino, through the baby redwood forest..

Unintentional Tasting

I hadn't intended to do a full tasting here but it was on a weekday and the staff were pretty bored so they talked me into it! Not sorry.

Lavender

Cloverdale, Geyserville, and Healdsburg are all positively covered in lavender -- fields of it everywhere! That's got to be intentional, Right?

Outdoor Tasking

Staff brought the wines out onto the veranda where one has an embarrassingly beautiful view of the Alexander Valley in the distance. Amazing.

Party on, Hanna!

I didn't plan this so I had to leave before the last two wines. The deeply chill staff refused to charge me. I left with a mixed case.

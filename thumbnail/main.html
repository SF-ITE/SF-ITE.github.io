Buena Vista Winery

Founded in 1857, and spanning from Sonoma and into Napa, Buena Vista is one of California's oldest wineries.

Francis Ford Coppola Winery

These photos are from a visit in the early Spring of 2021. It was a mild, sunny day, with temperatures in the mid 60's.

The Hanna Winery

88 acres planted with Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Malbec, and Petit Verdot. The soil and climate are ideal for red Bordeaux varieties

V. Sattui Winery

Vittorio Sattui started the business in 1885 after emigrating from Genoa, Italy. The winery shut in 1920 due to Prohibition and reopened in 1976.

USS Hornet (CV/CVA/CVS-12) is an Essex-class aircraft carrier built for the United States Navy (USN) during World War II.

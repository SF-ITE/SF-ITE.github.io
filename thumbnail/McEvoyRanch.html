McEvoy Ranch Winery

Red Hill Rd, Petaluma, California
https://www.mcevoyranch.com

From Olives To Grapes

McEvoy Ranch was founded in 1990 by Nan Tucker McEvoy and Nion McEvoy as an olive grove. They only started producing wine in 2006.

Pinot Wonderland

For a small, 28 acre winery, they produce an impressive variety and their Pinot Noirs are amazing, and I am not that fond of Pinot.

Relax Out Back

Behind the wine shop is a secluded little area where you can relax with a bottle and some snacks. Utterly beautiful!

Exploring the Property

They have a very open set up and one is pretty much free to wander about and explore. It is a lovely little place.

Gardens and Groves

This was one of the most peaceful, laid back places I have ever been. Perfect for walking off the wine and cheese. :)

Ideal Climate

West Marin County's Petaluma Gap offers the distinct climate and varied soil types that are conducive to making great wine.

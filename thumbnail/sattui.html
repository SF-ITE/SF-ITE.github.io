V. Sattui Winery

White Ln, St Helena, California
https://www.vsattui.com/

History

Vittorio Sattui started the business in 1885 after emigrating from Genoa, Italy. The winery shut in 1920 due to Prohibition and reopened in 1976.

Wine Shop

Remarkable selection and the average per bottle cost was about $40.00, which is not at all unreasonable for that level of quality.

The Castle

The architecture here is stunning. The place looks like a castle, all stone and built to last 1,000 years. The fountain makes a great chill site.

Wine Tasting Room

Beautiful old bar, but I gave it a miss and grabbed a bottle of Shiraz and one of Shiraz and wandered off to explore the winery.

Lunch Time

Took the wine and some cheese to the garden for lunch. Yes, I know about the chipmunk cheeks; I find it funny so I left it in

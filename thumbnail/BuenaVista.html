Buena Vista Winery

Old Winery Rd, Sonoma, California
https://buenavistawinery.com

A Long, Proud History

Founded in 1857, and spanning from Sonoma into Napa, Buena Vista is one of California's oldest wineries.

Agoston Haraszthy

The founder, a Hungarian immigrant named Agoston Haraszthy, was a bit of a character, giving himself the title, 'Count of Buena Vista'.

Hanging Out in Paradise

It is really easy to spend a full day here wandering around and dropping in on every tasting. It is a very chill vibe.

So Many Wines!

I tasted so many vintages that day I am embarrassed to admit I have no idea what I am drinking in the photo. 2019 Sauvignon Blanc?

Appellation Carneros

The Carneros appellation is considered the crossroads of both Napa and Sonoma Valley where the two major wine regions collide.

Breathing Room

This is February, 2021, thus the mask hanging off my wrist. It felt so good to get out of the City and into a wide-open space!

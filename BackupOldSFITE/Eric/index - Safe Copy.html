Eric Errickson - Welcome

Hi there! I'm Eric.

I am a software engineer and developer for SF-ITE.com in San Francisco. I write games and applications for Android, Linux, and Windows. I also have a weekly security and tech culture column at theITguy.gq. I have several UX/UI certifications and regularly consult with other development houses to tweak their IPs.

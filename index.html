Hi, I'm Eric

I spent more than twenty years as a programmer, and I used to literally lose sleep worrying about things breaking. Bugs haunted my dreams.

Not Anymore

Now I break things for fun and profit. At a high level, my new job is to push buttons and twist dials until machines do things they were never intended to do.

Changing Teams

In 2020 a lot of development work dried up and I had to make some tough decisions. I shut down my little business, and started learning how to be a cyber-criminal.

Lockdown Learning

I began to train breaking into devices and systems. My background was a huge advantage and soon I was winning CTFs and racking up certifications.

Ethical Hacking

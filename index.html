What We Do

Industry certified hardware and software engineers on-demand, 24/7 in the City.

iOS Apps and Games

We build iPhone and iPad apps from the ground up. We especially focus on iOS games in C++, but we develop B2B apps as well. Perhaps most importantly, we navigate the process of getting your application into the Apple Store/iTunes for you, including approval, credentials, and financials.

Android Apps and B2B

We develop a wide variety of Android applications directly in Java, and port applications to Android from practically every programming language in use today. Of course, we navigate the process of getting your application into the Google Play Store for you, including app approval and log-in credentials.

Windows 10 Development

Specializing in C# and the .NET ecosystem, we build native 64-bit applications for Microsoft Windows 10 and Server 2016 to client specifications. We storyboard, prototype, test and refine your concept, then iterate and test again until we produce your deliverables. We also offer backend support, as well as DB management and dedicated DBA services.

AWS and E3 Deployment

SF-ITE is an AWS Enterprise Partner, and we leverage that relationship to help our clients obtain the best rates on cloud services. We offer managed and un-managed EC2, Lightsail, Elastic Beanstalk, and other AWS services both annually and month to month. Database management and jQuery a specialty.

Google Ecosystem Migration

SF-ITE is proud to be a Google Cloud Platform Partner. We manage all aspects of cloud migration for clients new to the Google Ecosystem, from server and database relocation to handoff of local services. The list of services is far too long to detail here, but we support them all.

eCommerce Solutions

SF-ITE partners with Shopify, Braintree, and PayPal to offer: Custom Shopping Cart Development -- Payment Gateway Integration -- Custom Theme Design -- 3rd Party Apps -- SEO Solutions -- Online Marketplace Development -- Credit Card Payment Systems
